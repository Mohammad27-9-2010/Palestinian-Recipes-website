Falafel

Falafel is a deep-fried ball or patty made from ground chickpeas, herbs, and spices. It's a popular breakfast dish in Palestine.

  • 1 cup chickpeas
  • 1/2 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup parsley
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Maqluba

Maqluba is a traditional Palestinian dish made with rice, vegetables, and meat, cooked in a pot and flipped upside down when served.

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 lb chicken
  • 1 eggplant
  • 1 cauliflower
  • 1 onion
  • Spices (cinnamon, cardamom, bay leaves)

Arabic Coffee

Arabic coffee is a traditional drink in Palestine, often served with cardamom and sugar.

  • 1/4 cup finely ground coffee
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 tsp cardamom
  • Sugar to taste

Knafeh

Knafeh is a sweet pastry made with shredded phyllo dough, cheese, and a sugary syrup. It's a beloved dessert in Palestine.

  • 1 lb shredded phyllo dough
  • 1 lb akkawi cheese
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tsp rose water
